Curved top headstone with shaped shoulders. 3 kerbstones; 2 posts with horizontal crosses. Ivy leaves carved round the shape of the headstone and down the sides.

IN
Affectionate Remembrance
of
MARY ANN
BELOVED WIFE OF JOHN MARTIN
DIED JULY 20 1910 AGED 54
Also of
JOHN MARTIN,
DIED AUGUST 5. 1929, AGED 74
ALSO
ALLAN MARTIN,
DIED JULY 7 1915 AGED 25
JAMES WILLIAM MARTIN WORCS REGT.
KILLED IN ACTION JULY 3 1916, AGED 30
GEORGE ERNEST MARTIN M G CORPS.
KILLED IN ACTION SEP 20 1917, AGED 22
JOHN MARTIN GRENADIER GUARDS,
KILLED IN ACTION OCT 12 1917, AGED 24
JANE PHIPPS,
DIED JUNE 26 1920. AGED 42.
ALFRED MARTIN DIED APRIL 7 1923 AGED 39.
CHARLES HENRY MARTIN,
DIED APRIL 7 1925 AGED 45
CHILDREN OF THE ABOVE.

Burial Register: B446 B497 B641

Burial Register: B446 gives age as 55 years for Mary Ann Martin.
