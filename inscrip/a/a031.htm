IN
LOVING
REMEMBRANCE OF
HANNAH
WIFE OF THOMAS SOUCH
WHO DIED APRIL 23 1886
AGED 72 YEARS
" O HAPPY DAY THAT FIX'D MY CHOICE
ON THEE MY SAVIOUR AND MY GOD
WELL MAY THIS GLOWING HEART REJOICE
AND TELL ITS RAPTURES ALL ABROAD."
ALSO OF THOMAS SOUCH
WHO DIED JANUARY 13
1907. AGED 96 YEARS.
