Curved top headstone with shaped shoulders. (The footstone to this grave is probably MI reference A009.) Headstone has a quatrefoil in roundel with

IHS

First line of inscription carved following the curve of the headstone.

IN AFFECTIONATE REMEMBRANCE OF
ELIZABETH SMITH,
WHO DIED JULY 29TH 1890
AGED 75 YEARS.
"THE LORD IS MY SHEPHERD"
ALSO OF
WILLIAM SMITH,
HUSBAND OF THE ABOVE
WHO DIED AT EDMONTON
AUGUST 30TH 1890
AGED 75 YEARS.
"SO SOON PASSETH AWAY AND WE ARE GONE"

Burial Register: B176 B178
