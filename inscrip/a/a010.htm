Large stone Celtic cross on double plinth. Inscribed in 4 parts round ring starting at 09.00 as seen on clock.

IN + SOLIUS
+HONOREM+
SALVATORIS
+DOMINI+

All edges carved as ropework. Roundels in each point of cross and centre. On rear, carving as ropework and roundels but no inscriptions.

Upper plinth East face.

IN DULCEM MEMORIAM
FILIAE PERCARISSIMAE
MARGARET ANNE BANDINEL
NATAE XVI MARTII MDCCCLVI
RENATAE FEST DOM ASCENS PROX
DENATAE XIVo MAII MDCCCLIX
DEPOSITAE XVIIIo MENSES EIUSDEM
RESERRECTURAE IN DIE DOMINI
MOESTI SED FIDENTES EREXERE PARENTE

Upper plinth North face.

TAKE UP
THE CROSS
AND FOLLOW
ME

Upper plinth South face.

BLESSED
ARE THE DEAD
WHICH DIE
IN THE LORD

Upper plinth West face.

COME UNTO ME
ALL YE THAT LABOUR
AND ARE HEAVY LADEN
AND I WILL GIVE YOU
REST
