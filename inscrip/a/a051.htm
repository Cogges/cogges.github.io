Curved top headstone with shaped shoulders.

This is one of the stones that was uncovered when work was done to replace the path to the Chancel door. It was positioned on the East side of the Chancel door in November 2004.

In Memory of
ELIZ: the wife of
WILLIAM HANKS
Who died Oct ye 20 1778
Aged 66 Years.
