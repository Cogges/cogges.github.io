Curved top headstone with shaped shoulders. Tablet with flower holder and footstone. Quatrefoil in roundel on headstone with inscription

IHS

First line of inscription follows curve of headstone.

IN AFFECTIONATE REMEMBRANCE OF
CATHERINE
WIFE OF CHARLES HALEY
WHO DIED FEBY 14TH 1894.
AGED 63 YEARS
ALSO OF CHARLES HALEY
WHO DIED MARCH 11TH 1906
AGED 76 YEARS
"ROCK OF AGES CLEFT FOR ME,
LET ME HIDE MYSELF IN THEE.
LET THE WATER AND THE BLOOD
FROM THY RIVEN SIDE WHICH FLOW'D
BE OF SIN THE DOUBLE CURE
CLEANSE ME FROM ITS GUILT AND POWER"

Tablet with flower holder.

IN LOVING MEMORY
OF
CHARLES HALEY
DIED DECEMBER 30 1989
AGED 83 YEARS.
REST IN PEACE.

Footstone.
