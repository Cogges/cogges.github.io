Gothic top headstone with shaped shoulders. Engraved with clasped hands in an oval near top. Ivy leaves to right and left of inscription.

In Loving Memory . of
EDWARD LEWIS
WHO DIED AUGUST 26TH 1914
AGED 56 YEARS
ALSO OF
ANN LEWIS
WIFE OF THE ABOVE
WHO DIED APRIL 15TH 1923
AGED 64 YEARS.
"ON THE RESURRECTION MORNING
SOUL AND BODY MEET AGAIN;
NO MORE SORROW, NO MORE WEEPING,
NO MORE PAIN.

Burial Register: B481 B592
