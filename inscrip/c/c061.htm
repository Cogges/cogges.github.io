Small pedestal with flower holder in top. Shield shape to east face containing inscription.

IN
LOVING MEMORY OF
HARRY TERRY
DIED 14 MARCH 1917.
ALSO ALICE HIS WIFE
DIED 21 JULY 1917.
"AT REST."

Burial Register: B521 B523 A 2 month old baby, Daisy May Terry, is registered in Burial Register: number B522. No grave inscription found. Henry Ernest Terry's age at burial was 27 years, Alice May Terry's age was 28 years.
