Scroll top headstone on 2 plinths. 3 kerbstones and stone flower holder in shape of an urn.

On headstone:-

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
STEPHEN SMITH,
DIED AUG. 27. 1933, AGED 80 YEARS.
"PEACE, PERFECT PEACE."
ALSO OF HIS BELOVED WIFE,
SARAH
DIED MAY 2. 1934, AGED 82 YEARS.
"RE-UNITED."
ALSO OF THEIR ONLY SON,
STEPHEN WILLIAM,
KILLED IN FRANCE JUNE 17. 1915,
AGED 32 YEARS.
"GREATER LOVE HATH NO MAN THAN THIS, THAT
A MAN LAY DOWN HIS LIFE FOR HIS FRIENDS."

On upper plinth

ALSO OF THEIR DAUGHTER
LOUISA MARY
DIED NOV. 19. 1963, AGED 75 YEARS.
"IN GOD'S KEEPING."

Masons mark on east kerb stone.

BILES
WITNEY
