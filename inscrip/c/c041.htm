Curved top headstone on single low plinth.

IN MEMORY OF
ARTHUR LONG
PASSED AWAY FEB. 14TH 1928
AGED 83 YEARS.
AND OF RUTH HIS BELOVED WIFE
WHO DIED APRIL 7TH 1935
AGED 88 YEARS.
RE-UNITED.
ALSO GERTIE
THE LOVED WIFE OF FRANK LONG
FELL ASLEEP DEC 23RD 1929.

Burial Register: B624, B645, B711 Burial register gives full name of Gertie as Gertrude Mary Long aged 45 years.
