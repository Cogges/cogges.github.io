Scroll top headstone set inside kerbstone on single plinth. 4 kerbstones with 2 posts at foot end. Stone flower vase at centre of plot, and tablet at foot of grave. On headstone:

In loving memory of
MY BELOVED HUSBAND,
CHARLES WILLIAM LLOYD.
DIED MAY 29TH 1931,
AGED 52 YEARS.
"REST AFTER WEARINESS, PEACE AFTER PAIN,
SAFE IN GOD'S KEEPING, TILL WE MEET AGAIN."
ALSO OF LOUISA ANNIE,
WIFE OF THE ABOVE,
PASSED AWAY FEB. 12TH 1954
AGED 74 YEARS.
REUNITED

On tablet, 2 crosses at right and left top corners:

GWENDOLINE J COOPER
DIED 4TH FEB 1995
AGED 77 YEARS
AND HER HUSBAND
WILLIAM J COOPER
DIED 20TH DEC. 1999
AGED 83 YEARS.
