Celtic cross on single large plinth. Large double grave plot with kerbstones. Plain central stone tablet on grave with no inscription. East face of plinth:

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
DANIEL LEARY
WHO DIED JULY 8TH 1929
AGED 80 YEARS.
ALSO OF HIS SISTER
MARY LEARY
WHO DIED JULY 28TH 1935
AGED 80 YEARS.

Burial Register: B640, B714
