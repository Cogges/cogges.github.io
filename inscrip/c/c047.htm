Gothic top headstone with kerbstones. Carved roundel with:

IHS

Main inscription: first line of inscription follows shape of stone top.

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
WILLIAM GLAISTER,
WHO DIED JUNE 24TH 1913,
AGED 62 YEARS.
"GRANT HIM O LORD ETERNAL REST."
ALSO
HARRIETT ELIZA,
WIFE OF THE ABOVE,
WHO DIED AUGUST 2ND 1930,
AGED 72 YEARS.
"AT THE RISING UP OF THE SUN,
UNTIL THE GOING DOWN OF THE SAME,
AND IN THE MORNING
WE WILL REMEMBER THEM.

Burial Register: B471, B657
