Double plot with 4 kerbstones. Overgrown with weeds. Large cross on top of pedestal and plinth. Cross has inscribed in centre

IHS

Upper part of plinth east face:-

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
ELIZABETH MAWLE
WHO DIED JUNE 28TH 1910
AGED 70 YEARS
ALSO OF
JOSEPH MAWLE
HER BELOVED HUSBAND
AT REST NOVEMBER 15TH 1934
AGED 99 YEARS.

Lower plinth east face

"SURELY HE HATH BORNE OUR GRIEFS,
AND CARRIED OUR SORROWS."

Plinth south face

ALSO OF
ELIZA MAWLE
BORN MARCH 4TH 1877,
DIED SEPT. 1ST 1935.

Plinth north face

ALSO OF
MARGARET HELEN
MAWLE
BORN FEB. 24TH 1879,
DIED AUG. 11TH 1966.
