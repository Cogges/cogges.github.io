Curved top headstone with flat shoulders, 3 kerbstones and 2 posts. Overgrown.

Sacred to the memory
of
JAMES HARWOOD
WHO DIED SEPTEMBER 6TH 1917
AGED 74 YEARS.
ALSO MARIA, HIS WIFE
WHO DIED DECEMBER 18TH 1929
AGED 96 YEARS.
"AT REST WITH THE LORD."
