Large double plot with 4 kerbstones. Large cross on 3 plinths. 2 sloping tablets to right and left of centre of plot, both with integral flower holders. Flower holder to foot of grave. Centre of cross has in a roundel inscribed

IHS

Inscriptions on 3 levels of plinth and on tablets. Upper east face

IN
LOVING MEMORY
OF

Middle east face

JOHN FRANCIS MAWLE,
OF SPRING HILL,
WHO ENTERED INTO REST DEC. 28TH 1917,
AGED 50 YEARS.
"FATHER IN THY GRACIOUS KEEPING,
LEAVE WE NOW THY SERVANT SLEEPING."

Lower east face

ALSO OF HIS BELOVED WIFE
KATE EDITH MAWLE,
WHO DIED MARCH 22ND 1935,
AGED 66 YEARS

Middle north face

ALSO THEIR SON
GEORGE CYRIL
MAWLE
OF MANOR FARM
WHO DIED 15 AUG.1968

Right tablet

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
JOSEPH EDWARD
MAWLE

AGED 71 YEARS.

Left tablet

JOHN FRANCIS MAWLE
FARMER AND CONSERVATIONIST
DIED 4.6.98
AGED 59 YEARS
