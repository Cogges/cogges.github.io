Curved top headstone and 3 kerbstones.

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
WILLIAM ROWE TRESIDDER
WHO DIED AUGUST 27TH 1917,
AGED 74 YEARS.
ALSO OF ELIZA ANN, HIS WIFE,
WHO DIED FEBRUARY 19TH 1927,
AGED 86 YEARS.
ALSO OF WINIFRED FAIRBURN,
NIECE OF THE ABOVE
BORN 19 JUNE 1874.
DIED 2 MARCH 1949.
