Scroll top headstone on single plinth.

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
MY DEAR HUSBAND
ROBERT HADDEN
DIED OCT. 30 1929.
AGED 79 YEARS.
"A LOVING HUSBAND A FATHER DEAR
A FAITHFUL FRIEND LIES BURIED HERE.
HE SUFFERED LONG, NOW FREE FROM CARE
HE TRUSTED IN HIS SAVIOUR DEAR.
THEN DRY THOSE TEARS AND WEEP NO MORE,
FOR HE'S NOT LOST, BUT GONE BEFORE."
ALSO GEORGINA, HIS DEAR WIFE,
DIED DEC. 2. 1933,
AGED 82 YEARS.
"AT REST."

Burial Register: B643, B694
