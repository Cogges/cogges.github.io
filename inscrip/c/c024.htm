Celtic cross on three plinths. In roundel at centre of cross:

RIP

Upper plinth

in loving memory
of
ELIZABETH JANE

Middle plinth

THE DEARLY BELOVED WIFE OF
ALBERT BEECHEY
WHO DIED JULY 17TH 1913
AGED 49 YEARS

Lower plinth

ALSO OF
ALBERT BEECHEY,
DIED MARCH 7TH 1943
AGED 78 YEARS.

Burial Register: B472, B796 Burial register spells Elizabeth's surname - Beecher.
