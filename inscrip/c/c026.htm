In
loving memory
of
CHARLES HARRIS

Also of
ANN ZILLAH HARRIS
WIFE OF THE ABOVE
WHO DIED MAY 23RD 1896
AGED 83 YEARS.
also of
ANN ZILLAH BEDDING.

also of
GEORGE LONG
WHO DIED MAY 17TH 1895
AGED 76 YEARS.
