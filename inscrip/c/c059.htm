Scroll topped headstone. 4 kerbstones, overgrown. Inscription on headstone.

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
EMMA GILLETT
WHO PEACEFULLY PASSED AWAY
20TH FEBRUARY 1927
AGED 92 YEARS
ALSO OF
WILLIAM GILLETT
HER HUSBAND
WHO DIED AT SHEPHERDS BUSH
ON THE 12TH MAY 1882,
AGED 48 YEARS.
INTERRED IN HAMMERSMITH CEMETERY.

Burial Register: B616
