Gothic top headstone.

In
Loving Memory
of
EMMA,
WIFE OF WILLIAM COLLIER,
WHO DIED MARCH 29TH 1930,
AGED 82 YEARS.
"ROCK OF AGES CLEFT FOR ME."
Also of
WILLIAM COLLIER,
WHO DIED OCT. 26TH 1934,
AGED 81 YEARS.
"THY WILL BE DONE"

Burial Register: B647, B704 Burial register has William's surname spelt as Collins or Collis.
