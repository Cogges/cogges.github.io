Cross on flat top headstone with shaped shoulders, angled tablet at foot of headstone, flower holder, and flat tablet immediately adjacent to east. Inscription on headstone:

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
ALFRED HALL
WHO FELL ASLEEP
MAR 2 1920 AGED 72 YEARS.
Thy will be done
Also of LOUISA HIS WIFE
DIED MAR 6 1937 AGED 90 YEARS
“O receive my soul at last”

On sloping tablet:

ALSO OF DOROTHY LILIAN HALL MB BS
1928 - 1980

On flat tablet:

HER SISTER
LUCY SCOTT
1910 - 1992.

Burial Register: B558, B734, C261, C341
