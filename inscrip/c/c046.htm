Curved top headstone with 3 kerbstones. 2 stone flower vases. Arch of decorative carving following shape of stone; trefoil of leaves in centre of arch.

In ever loving memory of
EDWARD HARRIS
WHO PASSED AWAY MAY 12TH 1929
AGED 79 YEARS.
"HIS END WAS PEACE."
ALSO OF HIS BELOVED WIFE,
MARY
WHO DIED FEB. 26TH 1944
AGED 88 YEARS.
"THY WILL BE DONE."

Burial Register: B639, C005
