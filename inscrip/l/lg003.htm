A brass plaque commemorates the building of the lych gate.

1897.
TO THE GLORY OF GOD,
AND IN COMMEMORATION OF 60 YEARS REIGN OF
QUEEN VICTORIA,
THIS LYCH GATE WAS ERECTED BY HER
LOYAL SUBJECTS IN THE PARISH OF COGGS
OF STONE MADE READY BEFORE IT WAS BROUGHT
HITHER: SO THAT THERE WAS NEITHER HAMMER
NOR AXE, NOR ANY TOOL OF IRON HEARD.
1 KINGS: VI: 7
ED J U PAYNE, VICAR. H ROBBINS,
W M CANTWELL CH-WARDENS.
