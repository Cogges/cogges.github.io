A terracotta plaque on the inner wall commemorates the 60th year of the reign of Queen Victoria. It bears profile of the Queen, and the text:

VICTORIA, 60 YEARS QUEEN
OF GREAT BRITAIN AND IRELAND
EMPRESS OF INDIA.
CANADA, AUSTRALIA
NW ZEALAND, BURMAH
GIBRALR, MALTA, CYPRUS, EGYPT
AFRICA, W INDIES.
