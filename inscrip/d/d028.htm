Small curved top headstone, 3 kerbstones and 4 posts. Heavily overgrown with weeds. A number of letters have fallen away but inscription is fairly clear.

In [Lo]ving M[em]ory
of
CH[R]ISTOPHER CLARIDGE
ACCIDENTLY KILLED
AUGUST 30TH 195[5] AGED 16 YEARS
THE LORD IS MY SHEPHERD.

Burial Register: C094
