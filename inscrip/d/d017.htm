Flat top headstone with shaped shoulders, 3 kerbstones and 2 posts. Tablet to head end and flower holder to foot end.

Headstone inscription

IN LOVING MEMO[R]Y OF
A DEVOTED HUSBAND AND FATHER
HERBERT ROY MOSS
WHO FEL[L] ASL[E]EP JUNE 14TH 1961
AGED 55 YEARS.
ALSO OF
A DEVOTED WIFE AND MOTHER
EDNA MAUD
WHO FELL ASLEEP JUNE 8TH 1980
AGED 73 YEARS
"NOT MY WILL, O LORD, BUT THINE BE DONE"

On tablet:

ALSO THEIR SON
COLIN MICHAEL MOSS
WHO DIED 8TH FEB 1996
AGED 66 YEARS
REST IN PEACE

On east face of one pillar the mason's mark

BILES.
