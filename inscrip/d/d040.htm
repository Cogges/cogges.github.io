Flat top headstone with shaped shoulders.3 kerbstones and 2 posts. Engraving either side of inscription. Large tablet at head end of grave and square stone flower holder at foot. On headstone

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
ARTHUR DAVID REED
WHO PASSED AWAY 28TH APRIL 1950
AGED 76 YEARS.
LOVED AND REMEMBERED.
ALSO HIS WIFE
KATIE
WHO PASSED AWAY 28TH AUG 1977
AGED 96 YEARS.
RE-UNITED

On tablet

ALSO THEIR DAUGHTERS
GERTRUDE REED
WHO PASSED AWAY 5.FEB.1979
AGED 73 YEARS.
CONSTANCE REED
WHO PASSED AWAY 19.NOV.1997
AGED 88 YEARS.
