Pointed top headstone with shaped shoulders, 3 kerbstones, 2 posts, and square flower holder set centrally on grave. All monument made of red stone. Decorative engraving to both top corners of headstone.

IN
LOVING MEMORY OF
ERNEST LLOYD
WHO PASSED AWAY OCT. 10TH 1955
AGED 70 YEARS.
SLEEPING WHERE NO SHADOWS FALL.
ALSO OF HIS WIFE
GERTRUDE ELLEN
WHO PASSED AWAY JUNE 24TH 1969
AGED 86 YEARS.
RE-UNITED
