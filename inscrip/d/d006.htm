Curved top headstone, 3 kerbstones and 4 pillars. Flower block towards foot of grave. Worn lettering, was black now grey in places but readable.

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
A BELOVED HUSBAND AND FATHER
JOHN SHERBOURNE
WHO DIED 1ST DECEMBER 1967
AGED 65 YEARS
SLEEP AWHILE BELOVED; UNTIL I LIE WITH THEE,
THEN TOGETHER WITH OUR SAVIOUR,
WE WILL LIVE FOR ALL ETERNITY.
ALSO HIS WIFE
EVELYN ROSE
WHO DIED 8TH NOVEMBER 1975
AGED 77 YEARS.

Burial Register:C174, C219
