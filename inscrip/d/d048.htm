Flat top headstone with shaped shoulders. Crosses engraved either side of inscription. Some letters have fallen away and lettering has been repainted by hand. Square flower holder at head end of grave. On headstone

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
ARTHUR MOSS
DIED 12TH JULY 1948
AGED 55 YEARS.
AT REST.
ALSO HIS WIFE
ELLEN
DIED 16TH MAY 1975
AGED 84 YEA[R]S

On flower holder

ARTHUR MOSS
DIED 12 JULY 1948
AGED 55
