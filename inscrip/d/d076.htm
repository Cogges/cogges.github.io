Pointed top headstone with 2 tall posts either side of it. 3 kerbstones with 2 further posts and a square flower holder to head end of grave.

IN
LOVING MEMORY OF
JAMES WILLIAM STAINES
C. P. O. R. N. 1894 - 1919
WHO PASSED AWAY 1 APRIL 1940
AGED 59 YEARS.
- AT REST -
ALSO OF HIS WIFE
ELIZA HANNAH
WHO PASSED AWAY 20 APRIL 1965
AGED 79 YEARS.
RE-UNITED.
