Flat top headstone with shaped shoulders. Floral carving either side of inscription. 3 kerbstones and 2 capped posts. Square flower holder central on grave.

In Loving Memory of
JOSEPH MILES
WHO DIED FEB. 15TH 1937 . AGED 69 YEARS.
"AT REST"
ALSO OF HIS WIFE
WINIFRED JANE,
PASSED AWAY DEC. 19TH 1950,
AGED 79 YEARS.
"RE-UNITED"

Burial Register: B732 C064
