Flat top headstone with shaped shoulders, 3 kerbstones and 2 posts. Square flower holder to head end of grave. Sloping tablet at foot end of grave. The mason made an error in the first part of the inscription of the tablet and both the clear error and correction are transcribed here.

On headstone

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
RACHEL ANN BRIDGMAN
DIED SEPTEMBER 7TH 1952
AGED 77 YEARS.
ALSO OF HER HUSBAND
GEORGE EDWARD BRIDGMAN
DIED MAY 7TH 1955
AGED 84 YEARS.
"IN GOD'S KEEPING."

On tablet

CHRTIANA EVELYN
LLOYD
1899 - 1987

(transcribed here to show correction)

CHRISTIANA EVELYN
LLOYD
1899 - 1987
