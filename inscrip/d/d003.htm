Flat top headstone with shaped shoulders. 3 kerbstones with two short pillars at foot end. Stone block for flowers towards foot end of grave. Black lettering.

IN EVERLOVING MEMORY
OF
THOMAS ROBERT WRIGHT
CALLED TO HIGHER SERVICE
NOV. 29TH 1969, AGED 77 YEARS.
RESTING WHERE NO SHADOWS FALL
ALSO HIS WIFE
A[G]NES FLORENCE
D[IE]D AUG. 16TH 1980, AGED 8[6 or 9] YEARS
REUNITED.
