Flat top headstone, with one raised shoulder for engraved cross which extends down one side of inscription, set on single plinth with integral flower holder.

IN
LOVING MEMORY OF
THOMAS MOSS
DIED 6TH JUNE 1956
AGED 61 YEARS.
AND
THOMAS FREDERICK
HYATT
DIED 6TH MAY 1969
AGED 58 YEARS.
ALSO OF HIS WIFE
KATHLEEN MARION
REUNITED 19TH DEC. 1998
AGED 88 YEARS.

Burial Register: C101 C180 C397 Burial Register gives full name as Frederick Thomas Moss.
