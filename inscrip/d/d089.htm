Pointed top headstone on a plinth. 3 kerbstones and 2 posts. Square flower holder to foot end of grave.

IN
LOVING MEMORY OF
MARIA CLANFIELD

WHO PASSED PEACEFULLY AWAY
29 APRIL 1938, AGED 74 YEARS.
"AT REST."
ALSO OF HER BELOVED HUSBAND
JESSE CLANFIELD
WHO DIED 22 DECEMBER 1941
AGED 78 YEARS.
"RE-UNITED"
ALSO OF GEORGE WILLIAM
SON OF THE ABOVE
KILLED IN ACTION IN FRANCE
2 JUNE 1915, AGED 23 YEARS.
"IN THE MIDST OF LIFE, WE ARE IN DEATH."

Burial Register: B744 B787
