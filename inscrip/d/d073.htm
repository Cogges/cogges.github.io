Curved top headstone with shaped shoulders and 3 kerbstones. Cross engraved in roundel above inscription. Stone 8 sided flower holder to foot of grave and round metal flower vase to head end of grave.

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
ERNEST HALEY
WHO ENTERED INTO REST
7TH DECEMBER 1942
AGED 57 YEARS.
"TILL WE MEET AGAIN."
ALSO OF HIS BELOVED WIFE
LIZZIE FLORENCE
FELL ASLEEP 29TH AUGUST 1956
AGED 64 YEARS.
RE-UNITED
