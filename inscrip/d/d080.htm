Flat top headstone with shaped shoulders. Crosses engraved either side of inscription. 3 kerbstones.

IN SACRED MEMORY OF
JOSEPH RALPH HESTER.
WHO DIED FEBRUARY 28TH 1941.
AGED 68 YEARS.
ALSO OF JESSIE HIS WIFE
WHO DIED DECEMBER 24TH 1955
AGED 87 YEARS
"ABIDE WITH ME"
