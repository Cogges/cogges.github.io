Curved top headstone with curved mantel and sides to stone. 3 kerbstones and 2 posts. Sloping tablet to foot end of grave with integral flower holder. On headstone

IN
LOVING MEMORY OF
OUR DEAR MOTHER AND FATHER.
ESTHER RYMAN WELLER
DIED JULY 13. 1949, AGED 71.
AND
DANIEL WILLIAM WELLER
DIED JANUARY 3. 1953, AGED 73.
AT REST.

On tablet

IN LOVING MEMORY
OF A DEAR
MUM AND DAD
ELSIE GERTRUDE
SIMPSON
DIED 10TH MARCH 1986
AND
CYRIL JOSEPH
SIMPSON
DIED 15TH MARCH 1990
REST IN PEACE

Burial Register: C049 C078 C304 C330 Burial Register: gives Elsie Gertrude Simpson's age as 73 years and Cyril Joseph Simpson's age as 81 years at the time of burial.
