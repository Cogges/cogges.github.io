East Window.

In loving remembrance of CHARLES BAILEY, many years
C' Warden of this Parish who died May 8, 1868; this
window was erected by his faithful wife, MARY BAILEY.

Bible scene at each pane of the window depicting Biblical miracles of healing.

(a) Moses and the Brazen Serpent

(b) Naaman the Leper

(c) King Jeroboam and unnamed prophet ( I Kings 13)

(d) The Invalid at the Pool of Bethesda

(e) The Blind Man at the Pool of Siloam

(f) The Deaf and Dumb Man

Bible text of Biblical miracles of healing on each of the three main panes of the window.

"Arise, take up thy bed".
"Go wash in the Pool of Siloam".
"Ephphatha".
