Wall tablet. Marble. Cartouche in marble with pinkish tinge. Black oval inscription tablet is framed in a wreath of leaves and ribbons, surmounted by a coat-of-arms; either side two cherubs, one blowing a pipe and the other holding an hour-glass. The whole cartouche mounted on swags. Incised white-painted lettering.
