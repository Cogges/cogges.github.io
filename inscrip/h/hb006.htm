Inscription HB006 Index Area Plan MI Plan H < Prev. Next >

Wall tablet. Lozenge shaped black marble tablet. Writing contained only in the upper half of the lozenge.

Here
Lieth ye body
of FRANCIS BLAKE
Esqr: who was buried
ye 25 of March 1692
And MARY his Wife ye 17
of September 1691
