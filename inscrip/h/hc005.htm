Stone wall tablet. Scrolled pediment with scallop shell in centre above the oval inscription tablet; swags of fruit and drapery hang on either side; supported on apron of an angel's head surrounded by gold wings. The central inscription tablet which is surrounded by a laurel wreath, is divided into halves by a broad creamy-white band. Inscription in poor condition.
