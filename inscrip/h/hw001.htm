WAR MEMORIAL. Oak. Incised inscription, with the verse below in Italic writing.

IN
MEMORIAM
1914-1918
J.CHAPMAN. G.W.C.CLANFIELD.
W.COX. A.HAGGITT. C.S.HARRIS.
J.HARRIS. W.HARRIS. L.E.J.HINTON.
A.C.HORNE. T.KEENE. A.H.MILES.
E.MILES. A.PRATLEY. T.PURBRICK.
G.STANLEY. A.E.WALKER. G.WALKER.
A.WOODCOCK. M.R.WOODCOCK.
F.YOUNG. H.B.YOUNG.
1939-1945
N.L.BASTON. F.W.LOOKER. R.MOORE.
They shall not grow old, as we that are left grow old:
Age shall not weary them, nor the years condemn.
At the going down of the sun and in the morning
We will remember them.

The following is a list containing the Christian names of each person named above. The list was provided by Mr Pratley.

1914-18
Jim Chapman. George W.C. Clanfield.
William Cox. Aubrey Haggit. C. Stan Harris.
John Harris. William Harris. Lewis E.J. Hinton.
Albert Horne. Tom Keene. Albert H. Miles.
Ernest Miles. Archie Pratley. Tom Purbrick.
George Stanley. Albert E. Walker. George Walker.
Andrew Woodcock. M. Robert Woodcock.
Frank Young. Hariph B. Young.
1939-45
N. Leslie Baston. Frank W. Looker. Ronnie Moore

June 24th, 1950, while serving with the O.B.L.I. in Cyprus
Gordon William Buckingham
