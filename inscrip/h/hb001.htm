Wall tablet. Rectangular black, possibly polished, slate wall tablet, bordered by floral and foliage decoration; surmounted by coat of arms, supported by stylised beasts. Inscription deeply incised.

In Memory of
WILLIAM BROOKS
who Died July ye 19th, 1766.
Aged 63 Years.

Sculptor's name (slightly damaged).

Sculptor - Thos. HAYNES, Witney
