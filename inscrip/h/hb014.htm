Floor slab with worn black surface. Rectangular slab, with border of 6.5cm decorated with incised rosettes in roundels.

In
Memory of
MARY WRIGHT
Daur of WILLIAM WRIGHT
of Middle-Field FARM,
who died May 17th 1776
Aged 32 Years
Hopes of a Joyful Refurrection.
