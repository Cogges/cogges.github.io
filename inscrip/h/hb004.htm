Wall tablet. Large mostly marble, with black slate inscription tablet, divided into three by marble acanthus leaves, surmounted by moulded shelf on which rest three Portrait Busts, one woman and 2 men, under a scroll pediment with heraldic achievement. The Blake family acquired Cogges Manor in second half of 17th century.
