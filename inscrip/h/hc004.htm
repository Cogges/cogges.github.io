Wall plaque in metalwork. Rectangular metal wall plaque supported on either side, by angels. Inscription in Gothic writing with some capitals in red (underlined):

This Tablet was placed here by Friends &
Parishioners to the memory of MARY BAILEY
widow of CHARLES BAILEY, sometime
Churchwarden, in memory of whom she gave
the East window of this Church
She fell asleep Febry 4th 1886 , aged 85 years.
