Flat top headstone with shaped shoulders topped with Celtic Cross on narrow plinth.

In
Memory of
JOHN TURNER
for 35 years Clerk of this parish
died 27th February 1859
Aged 75 Vears
-
Also of HANNAH his wife
died 27 February 1861
Aged 7[3 or 5] Vears.

Burial Register: A578, A618
