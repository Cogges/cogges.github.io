Octagonal top to pointed stone, with fleuree carved cross in octagon.

IN MEMORY
OF
RICHARD OSBORNE
WHO DIED
JANUARY 15TH 1847
AGED 47 YEARS
ALSO SARAH OSBORNE HIS WIFE
MANY YEARS THE MISTRESS OF THE INFANT SCHOOL
OF NEWLAND
WHO DIED OCTOBER 15th 1878
AGED 78 YEARS
