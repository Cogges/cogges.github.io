Curved top headstone. Cherub and wings lightly carved above inscription.

Sacred
To the Memory of
ANN, wife of
JOSEPH DUTTON
Officer of Excise, Witney
who departed this life
Novemr 28th 1825
IN THE 48TH YEAR OF HER AGE
Farewell my Hufband and Children dear
My friends and Neighbours too
The Lord was pleafed to take me home
As [___________] this [________]
Dear Hufband bring my children up
To fear the Lord [_____]
Untill you come to sleep with me
Within this [___] Grave.
