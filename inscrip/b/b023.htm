SACRED
TO THE MEMORY OF
MARY ANN
OSBORN
who died Aug 6 1832
AGED 7 YEARS [_] MONTHS
ALSO IN MEMORY OF
JOHN OSBORN
WHO DIED NOV 23 1862
AGED 67 YEARS
ALSO OF MARY ANN WIFE OF
THE LATE JOHN OSBORN
WHO DIED SEP 13 1869
AGED [75] YEARS.

Footstone inscription.

MAO
1832
J. O. 1862
