Gothic top headstone with trefoil shape carved inside with lily of the valley.

IN LOVING MEMORY
OF
PHILIP GRAHAM LANE,
WHO FELL ASLEEP
MARCH 30TH 1912,
AGED 59 YEARS.
"UNTIL THE DAY BREAK, AND THE
SHADOWS FLEE AWAY."
Also
IN AFFECTIONATE REMEMBRANCE OF
JANE PRATLEY LANE,
WIFE OF THE ABOVE,
WHO ENTERED INTO REST
OCTOBER 21ST 1926,
AGED 75 YEARS.
"MY PEACE I GIVE TO YOU."

Mason's mark on lower north edge of stone:

BILES
WITNEY
