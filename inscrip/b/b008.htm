Curved top headstone with deeply carved Celtic cross surrounded by an arch of decorative carving.

In Loving Remembrance of
RICHARD HENRY HOLLIS.
WHO PASSED AWAY AUGUST 31ST 1921,
AGED 69 YEARS.
"GRANT HIM, LORD, ETERNAL REST."
Also of
HANNAH,
WIFE OF THE ABOVE
WHO DIED OCTOBER 12TH 1923,
"THE LORD HIMSELF THE SOUL WILL KEEP,
GIVING OUR BELOVED SLEEP."

Burial Register: B574 B596 Burial Register gives age of Hannah Hollis as 76 years.
