Round top headstone with shaped shoulders. Flower holder, and tablet at head end of grave area. Some decorative carving in roundel at top of stone. Evidence of black paint in carving of inscription.

IN
AFFECTIONATE REMEMBRANCE
OF
MARY, WIFE OF
RICHARD HOLLIS.
WHO DIED JULY 25TH 1873
AGED 59 YEARS.
ALSO OF RICHARD HOLLIS.
WHO DIED JANUARY 9TH 1895
AGED 87 YEARS.

On tablet

MAUD HOLLIS
1894 - 1971
WILLIAM H HOLLIS
1884 - 1979
