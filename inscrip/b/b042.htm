Mantle top chest tomb. Inscription on top surface.

TO THE MEMORY OF
JOHN HOLLIS
who died April 19 1857
AGED 64 YEARS
Also of EDWARD son of
JOHN and MARTHA
HOLLIS
WHO DIED MARCH 2[_] 1822 AGED 1 MONTH
Also of MARTHA wife of
JOHN HOLLIS
WHO DIED NOV 18 1878
AGED 8[7 or 9] YEARS
