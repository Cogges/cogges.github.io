Flat top headstone with shaped shoulders. Roundel at top of stone with cross carved. First line of inscription forms an arch over the roundel, following the shape of the stone.

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
JAMES TURNER.
WHO DIED JAN 18TH 1903
AGED 59 YEARS
ALSO OF
SARAH TURNER
WHO DIED MAY 26TH 1908
AGED 57 YEARS.
PEACE PERFECT PEACE
WITH LOVED ONES FAR AWAY
