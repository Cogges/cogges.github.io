Pointed top headstone with shaped shoulders. Roundel at top with flower carved inside.

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
CHARLES LONG
WHO DIED SEPTEMBER 17TH 1895
AGED 7[5 or 8] YEARS.
ALSO OF AMELIA
WIFE OF THE ABOVE
WHO DIED JANUARY 15TH 1903
AGED 85 YEARS.
"BLESSED ARE THE DEAD WHICH DIE
IN THE LORD."
