Headstone (red) with flat top and shaped shoulders, 3 red kerbstones. Tablet (red) at head end of grave and flower block towards foot of grave. Flowers carved into headstone and a cross at the top.

IN
LOVING MEMORY OF
MARY ANN CLAPTON
DIED OCTOBER 13. 1938
AGED 78 YEARS.
LOVED ONE SLEEPING.
ALSO HER MUCH BELOVED DAUGHTER
IDA MAY BARNES
WHO PASSED AWAY 24. DECEMBER 1976 AGED 73.

On the tablet:

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
CLIFFORD FRANK BARNES, M.B.E.
DIED 27TH MARCH 1978.
AGED 74 YEARS.
MISSED BY HIS FAMILY
AND RADIO AMATEUR FRIENDS, WORLDWIDE.
