Plinth with missing cross or similar feature. 4 kerbstones broken and all plot overgrown by mature tree. Inscription on upper surface of plinth and east face of plinth. Not known if there is more inscription, this was transcribed by digging 4 inches below soil level.

Upper surface of plinth

IN LOVING MEMORY OF

East face of plinth

ANNIE JOHNSON
THE DEARLY LOVED CHILD OF
CHARLES W. AND MARY A BATES
OF SOWERBY BRIDGE, YORKS
BORN 7 FEB 1899 DIED JUNE 19 190[2]
