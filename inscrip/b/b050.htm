Curved top headstone with shaped shoulders. Trefoil carved with flowers and an arch of leaves following the arch of the stone.

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
HARRIETT
THE BELOVED WIFE OF
EDWARD ROWLES
(OF NEWLAND)
WHO SUDDENLY FELL ASLEEP
SEPTEMBER 7TH 1891
AGED 68 YEARS
"SORROW VANQUISHED, LABOUR ENDED
JORDAN PASSED"
ALSO OF
EDWARD ROWLES
WHO AFTER LONG SUFFERING
ENTERED ONTO REST
JUNE 29TH 1902
AGED 75 YEARS
OVER THE RIVER THEN HAPPY FOR EVER
IN THAT BEAUTIFUL LAND ON HIGH.

Burial Register: B191, B341
