Flat topped headstone with shaped shoulders. Carving around a cross, piercing the stone. Carved lettering showing traces of having been painted black. (The footstone to this grave is probably MI reference B011.)

In loving Memory of
WILLIAM JOHN WEARRING
WHO DIED JANY 15TH 1884
AGED 35 YEARS.
ALSO OF
FRED ALBAN BRICKNELL WEARRING
SON OF THE ABOVE
WHO DIED MAY 31ST 1882 AGED 6 YEARS
A FATHER KIND, A HUSBAND DEAR,
TO EVERY FRIEND, A FRIEND [S]INCERE
"HIS END WAS PEAC[E]"
ALSO OF
JANE WEARRING
WIFE OF THE ABOVE
WHO DIED SEP. 17TH 1928,
AGED 78 YEARS.

Burial Register: B083 B100 B631 Burial Register gives spelling of Fred A B's surname as Wearing.
