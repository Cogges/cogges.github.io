Curved top headstone with shaped shoulders, 3 kerbstones and 2 posts. Leaves carved around outer edge of face of stone and a cross in a roundel near the top.

In Loving Memory Of
SARAH ELLEN
THE BELOVED WIFE OF JOHN CANTWELL
WHO PASSED AWAY MAY 17TH 1899
AGED 46 YEARS.
"TO LIVE IN HEARTS WE LEAVE BEHIND
IS NOT TO DIE."
Also of
JOHN CANTWELL
WHO PASSED AWAY APRIL 25TH 1932
IN HIS 81ST YEAR.
"AT REST"

Burial Register: B303 B673
