Curved top headstone with shaped shoulders on base plinth with integral flower holder. Leaves engraved at top corners of headstone.

IN
LOVING MEMORY
OF
PETER SMALLWOOD
WHO DIED 21ST FEBRUARY 1987
AND
BETTY DOREEN SMALLWOOD
WHO DIED 3RD MAY 1987.
REST PEACEFULLY TOGETHER.

Burial Register: C307 C308 Burial Register gives P Smallwood age as 70 years and B Smallwood age as 61 years.
