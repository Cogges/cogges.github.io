Curved top headstone with shaped shoulders. Front of stone engraved as open book under a floral decoration. Set on base plinth with integral flower holder.

Inscription on left side of book.

IN
LOVING
MEMORY OF
ERNEST
THOMAS
LEWIS
WHO DIED
19 APRIL 1976
AGED 70 YEARS.
A LOVED ONE
SLEEPING.

Right side no inscription.

Burial Register: C220
