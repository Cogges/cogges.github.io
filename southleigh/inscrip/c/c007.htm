Wooden lectern. Angel in full armour standing on decoratively carved pillar and holding up the book rest above his head.

Inscription in a brass plaque on the book rest.

THIS LECTERN IS DEDICATED AS A
GRATEFUL THANK-OFFERING FOR 3 SONS
PRESERVED IN THE GREAT WAR 1914 - 1918
ARTHUR GERARD EAST
CAPT. R.A.M.C.
MICHAEL EDMUND EAST
LIEUT. R.N.
RUPERT MOULTRIE EAST
LIEUT. R.G.A.
"MY GOD HATH SENT HIS ANGEL AND HATH
SHUT THE LIONS' MOUTHS THAT THEY HAVE NOT
HURT ME. " Dan. VI. 22.
