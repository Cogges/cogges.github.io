Large floor slab with 4 corners decorated with quadrants containing leaves.

In Memory
of ELIZABETH the wife of
THOMAS HARPAR who
departed this life ye 9 day of
Septbr. 1758 Aged 68 years
She was a good wife, and a Indulgent
Mother, a good Neighbour and good to
all other, and when the Lord did please
for her to call, she resigned her self to him
and left us all

Burial Register: yes.
