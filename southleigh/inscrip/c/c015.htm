Stone floor slab with corners incised with oak leaves. Inscription in oval area.

Dark lettering on pale stone .

Here Lyeth
the body of JOHN
ye son of WILLIAM and
HANNAH SMITH. Who died
ye 25 day of March 1731.
Aged 16 years
Heark dont you hear his
sweet delightful voyce
Saying Frinds weep not
but see that yee
rejoyce

Burial Register: yes.
