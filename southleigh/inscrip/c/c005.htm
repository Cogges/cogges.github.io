Wooden Benefaction board. Pale surface with dark lettering.

BENEFACTORS
to the POOR of SOUTHLEIGH
Sr THOS GORE Knt gave Ten -
Pounds the interest to be applied
to their use forever.
JNO HART Gent gave Fifty Pounds
the interest to be laid out annually
in Coats at Christmas forever.
Mr LAWRENCE BETTS gave Five
Pounds the interest to be distributed
at Easter forever.
Mr THOS GUY gave Ten Pounds.
With the forenamed Sums, and
Two Pounds given by Mr JOHN-
SPIER, a purchase of Land was made
at Hailey, in the Parish of Witney.
Mr RICHD TALBUT gave Ten -
Pounds, The interest annually to be
distributed in Bread, the last Sun -
day in January forever.
