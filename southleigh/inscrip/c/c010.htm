East Window, stained glass, in 4 panels. The first line in each panel is the Bible text and the second line is the memorial inscription. Panels numbered from left to right.

Panel 1. Upper line.

O LAMB OF GOD

Panel 2. Upper line.

THAT TAKEST AWAY

Panel 3. Upper line.

THE SINS OF THE WORLD

Panel 4. Upper line.

HAVE MERCY UPON US

Panel 1. Lower line.

To the Glory of God and in Loving Memory of

Panel 2. Lower line.

SAMUEL and HANNAH DRUCE

Panel 3. Lower line.

this window is presented to the church of

Panel 4. Lower line.

St James South Leigh by their children

Glaziers mark on panel 4.

O'CONNOR, LONDON 1871
