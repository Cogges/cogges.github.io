Pointed decorative top headstone with shaped shoulders. Leger stone heavily overgrown.

IN MEMORY OF
RICHARD HOMAN,
WHO DIED JAN - 9 - 1829
AGED 80 YEARS.
ALSO OF ANN HIS WIFE
WHO DIED AUG - 9 - 1852
AGED 84 YEARS.
ALSO OF
GEORGE HOMAN,
THEIR SON
WHO DIED JULY [__] 1872
AGED 61 YEARS

Burial Register: A078, A231, A378. Register has Oman as surname for Richard Homan.
