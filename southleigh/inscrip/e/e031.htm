Decorative cross on a pointed headstone. Left shoulder broken away. Decorative right shoulder. Footstone decorative cross and shoulders. Large shrub growing on grave.

IN
MEMORY OF
EMMA WIFE OF
STEPHEN CANTWELL.
WHO DIED FEBRUARY 18TH 1877

MY TIME WAS SHORT [___]
MY DEAR REDEEMER CALLETH ME AWAY

Footstone.

E C
1877
