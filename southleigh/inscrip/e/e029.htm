Double plot with cross on 3 plinths. 4 kerbstones and 4 cross topped pillars. Square flower holder to centre of grave. Cross has a stone bird on one arm and ivy carved on the upright.

Upper plinth.

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
EMMA MARY ANN
WOODROW.

Middle plinth.

DIED MAY 6TH 1931, AGED 68 YEARS
A FAITHFUL WIFE A MOTHER DEAR,
A LOVING WIFE, IN MEMORY DEAR
HER LOVING WORK ON EARTH IS DONE
WE WEEP, BUT SAY GOD'S WILL BE DONE

Lower plinth.

CHARLES THOMAS WOODROW
DIED FEBRUARY 11TH 1934
AGED 71 YEARS
DUTY WELL DONE. RACE WELL RUN,
BATTLE WELL WON, THEN COMES REST.

On south east pillar.

UNDERWOOD
FORTUNE GRN
N. W.

Burial Register: A662, A665.
