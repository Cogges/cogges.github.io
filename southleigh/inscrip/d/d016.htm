To The Memory of
JOHN BARTLETT.
who died June 27th
1839
Aged 96 Years

JOHN [& S]ARAH BARTLETT
Who Died Feb 24 18[__]

Burial Register: A137, A142. Register gives year of death of William Bartlett as 1840, aged 66.
