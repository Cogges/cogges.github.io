Curved top headstone with shaped shoulders. 2 cherub's heads and engraving of leaves, flowers and fruit all around the west face of the stone.

Inscription on west face.

Underneath [lie the]
Remains of THOMAS ISAAC
EB[__]R and MARY FRANCES
Sons and Daughter of RICHARD and
RUTH FRANCES of this Parish
[_____]
Also the remains of RUTH FRANCES
their daughter [___]
[___] 1775

Inscription on east face.

Underneath lie the Remains
of JOSEPH son of RICHARD
and RUTH [FRANCES] who
died Sept ye 26 1777
Aged 12 years and 7 months
