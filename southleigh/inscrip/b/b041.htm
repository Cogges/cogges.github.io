Pointed top headstone with shaped shoulders. Deeply carved lozenge with a cross inside it. Heavy growth of lichen. Footstone with pointed top and shaped shoulders.

In E[____]ing Remembrance
of
WILLIAM HARRIS
WHO DEPARTED THIS LIFE
MARCH 5TH 1909,
AGED 79 YEARS.
"HE HATH DELIVERED MY SOUL IN PEACE."

Footstone.

W. H.
1909
