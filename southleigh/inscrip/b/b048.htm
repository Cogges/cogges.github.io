Cross on a stepped shoulder headstone. Footstone with a curved top. Cross has been inscribed with

IHS

Inscription on headstone.

+
DINAH HARRIS
FELL ASLEEP
OCT 25. 1872
AGED 71 YEARS
"HER END WAS PEACE"

At base of headstone.

THE YEARS ROLL ROUND AND STEAL AWAY
THE BREATH THAT FIRST THEY GAVE
WHAT E'RE WE DO WHERE'RE WE ARE
WE'RE TRAVELLING TO THE GRAVE

On footstone.

D. H
1872

Burial Register: A381.
