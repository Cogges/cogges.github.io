Pointed top headstone with 3 kerbstones and 2 pillars. Square flower holder toward foot of grave. Roundel in lower half of headstone with a cross carved in it. A flower carved to each top corner of the headstone.

Inscription on East kerb.

YOU HAVE SHOWN US THE WAY MOTHER
BY A LIFETIME OF GIVING TO OTHERS
GOD BLESS YOU.

Inscription on headstone.

IN
LOVING MEMORY OF
JULIA ADA JEFFREYS
BORN 30TH JUNE 1889
CALLED TO REST 2ND JUNE 1969
TOGETHER AGAIN IN THE FULLNESS OF TIME
ALSO OF HER HUSBAND
SANDERSON JEFFREYS
BORN 1885
CALLED TO REST 29TH JULY 1977
RE-UNITED

Burial Register: A770, A787.
