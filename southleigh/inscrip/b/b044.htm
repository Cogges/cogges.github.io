Curved top headstone with shaped shoulders.

In
Affectionate Remembrance
of
ELIZABETH.
THE BELOVED WIFE OF
ISAAC BROGDEN,
WHO DEPARTED THIS LIFE JUNE 13TH 1890
AGED 62 YEARS.
ALSO THE FOLLOWING CHILDREN OF THE ABOVE
JAMES, DIED NOV 6TH 1864. AGED 2 YEARS
LUKE, DIED JAN 8TH 1866. AGED 5 MONTHS
AGUSTINE, DIED FEB 27TH 1871. AGED 5 MONTHS
ISAAC JAMES, DIED MAR 19TH 1879 AGED 12 YEARS
ISAAC BROGDEN.
DIED JAN. 9TH 1923, IN HIS 91ST YEAR.
"FOR EVER WITH THE LORD."

Single line inscribed at base of stone.

GOD HATH GIVEN TO US ETERNAL LIFE.

Burial Register: A318, A322, A365, A419, A628.
