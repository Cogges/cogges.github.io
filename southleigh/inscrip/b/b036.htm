Flat top headstone with shaped shoulders and flat top foot stone with shaped shoulders. Headstone is leaning forward.

IN
LOVING REMEMBRANCE OF
MARY ANN WIFE OF
THOMAS RUSSELL,
DIED SEPTEMBER 16TH 1887.
AGED 54 YEARS.
THE LORD GAVE AND THE LORD HATH TAKEN AWAY
BLESSED BE THE NAME OF THE LORD.
ALSO OF
THOMAS RUSSELL,
WHO DIED NOVEMBER 15TH 1895
AGED [6]4 YEARS
AS IN ADAM ALL DIE EVEN SO IN CHRIST
SHALL ALL BE MADE ALIVE.

Inscription on footstone.

MAR
1887
TR
1895

Burial Register: A480, A525.
