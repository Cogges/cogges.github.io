Curved top headstone.

IN MEMORY
OF
WILLIAM WIDDOWS
WHO DIED SEP. 3. 1864
AGED 79 YEARS
ALSO OF
JANE WIDDOWS, HIS WIFE
WHO DIED MARCH 15TH 1885
AGED 92 YEARS.
THOU SHALT COME TO THY GRAVE
IN A FULL AGE, LIKE AS A SHOCK OF
CORN COMETH IN IN HIS SEASON
JOB V 26
THESE STONES WERE ERECTED
AS A TRIBUTE OF AFFECTION
BY CHILDREN OF THE ABOVE

Burial Register: A315, A785.
