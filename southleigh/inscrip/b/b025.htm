Damaged memorial. Celtic cross broken and leaning against 2 plinths. Inscription on plinths.

Upper plinth.

In Loving Memory
ELEANOR BRADSTREET
FELL ASLEEP
MARCH 30TH 1910

Lower plinth.

Angels bear thee to the land
Where the towers of Sion rise
Safely lead thee by the hand
[__] the fields of [_unreadable_]

Burial Register: A585. Register gives age as 69.
