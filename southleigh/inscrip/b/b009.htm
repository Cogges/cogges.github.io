Cruciform coped stone. Inscription on south side of stone. RAF Insignia on east side.

IN LOVE REMEMBER OUR DEAR SON PILOT OFFICER ERIC WRIGHT BLACKWELL, R. A. F. KILLED ON ACTIVE SERVICE WHILST FLYING 28 OCTOBER 1940. AGED 28.

Burial Register: A692.

This burial is recorded by the Commonwealth War Graves Commission.
