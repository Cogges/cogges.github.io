Gothic top headstone with 3 kerbstones. Roundel carved to top of headstone and a cross carved within the roundel.

In Affectionate Remembrance
of
ARTHUR BURDEN,
(BLIND ARTHUR)
WHO PASSED AWAY FEB. 25TH 1924,
AGED 49 YEARS.
"Lead, Kindly Light."

Inscribed at base of stone.

ERECTED BY RELATIVES AND PARISHIONERS
AS A TOKEN OF GREAT ESTEEM.

Burial Register: A635.
