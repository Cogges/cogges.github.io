Curved top headstone and footstone with ledger stone between. No inscription seen on ledger stone. On headstone and footstone inscription deeply incised and painted black.

Inscription on headstone.

In Memory of
WILLIAM GILLETT
who died May 15th 1856;
Aged 67 years.
And of MARY his Wife
who died March 29th
1857;
Aged 74 Years

Inscription of footstone.

W.G
1856
M.G
1857

Burial Register: A262, A270.
