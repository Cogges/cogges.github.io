Pointed top headstone on a plinth with flower holder to the right of the plinth. A rose is carved over a cross on the headstone. Also a plastic grave marker with metal plaque.

Inscription on headstone.

In Loving Memory
of a dear wife
PHIL TIPPING
who fell asleep
4th May 2002
aged 74 years

Inscription on the grave marker.

SARAH PHILOMENA
TIPPING
DIED 4TH MAY 2002
AGED 74 YEARS
