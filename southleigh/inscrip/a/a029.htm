Flat top headstone with left shoulder shaped. Cross carved down left side of headstone which is set on a single plinth with an inset flower holder.

IN
EVER LOVING MEMORY
OF
HERBERT GREEN
BORN MARCH 14TH 1885
DIED DECEMBER 29TH 1966.
SAFE IN THE ARMS OF JESUS
ALSO HIS WIFE
ALICE ELSIE
BORN FEB. 11TH 1888
DIED OCT 20TH 1973
REUNITED
