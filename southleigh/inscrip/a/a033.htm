Coffin shaped chest tomb with ledger stones either side. No inscriptions seen on ledger stones.

South slope of lid.

SACRED TO THE MEMORY OF WILLIAM GILLETT
WHO DIED SEP 10 1870 AGED [_]0 YEARS
ALSO OF ANN WIFE OF THE ABOVE
WHO DIED FEB [_1] 1886 AGED 71 YEARS

North slope of lid.

[_N] MEMORY OF LOUISA ELLEN
DAUGHTER OF WILLIAM AND ANN GILLETT WHO DIED NOV 12 1845
AGED 1 YEAR 8 MONTHS
ALSO OF CHARLES THEIR SON WHO DIED JUNE 2[5] 1863
AGED 2[3] YEARS

South side of tomb.

NORAH GILLETT,
DIED APRIL 21. 1931,
AGED 82 YEARS.

North side of tomb.

[______]
[DAU]G[H]TER OF WILLIA[M] [_] A[NN] GILLETT.
WHO DIED DECR 21ST 1899, [__D] [5]6 YEARS

Burial Register: A176, A308, A360, A468, A540, A661. Register gives William Gillett aged 60. For North side inscription, the name Louisa Ellen corresponds to the date Dec 1899, aged 53 years.
