Double plot. A cross set on 3 plinths and surrounded by 4 kerbstones.

West face Upper plinth.

IN
LOVING MEMORY
OF

West face Middle plinth.

JOHN NORMAN
ELDEST SON OF
JOHN AND EVA BRYAN

West face Lower plinth.

WHO DIED APRIL 23RD 1903
AGED 13 YEARS.

North face Upper plinth.

ALSO OF

North face Middle plinth.

JOHN BRYAN
WHO DIED NOV 4TH 1913
AGED 60 YEARS.

North face Lower plinth.

"COME UNTO ME, ALL YE THAT ARE
WEARY AND HEAVY LADEN."

Burial Register: A554, A602.
