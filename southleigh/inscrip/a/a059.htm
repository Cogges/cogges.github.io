Curved top headstone with shaped shoulders. 3 kerbstones with 2 pillars. Square stone flower holder to head end of grave. Inscription on headstone.

IN
LOVING MEMORY OF
JOHN HARRIS
DIED AUGUST 27TH 1956
AGED 79 YEARS.
ALSO OF EMILY HIS WIFE
DIED DECEMBER 4TH 1959
AGED 82 YEARS.
GRANT THEM LORD ETERNAL REST.

Burial Register: A745, A752.
