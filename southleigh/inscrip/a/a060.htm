Decorative top headstone with shaped shoulders. Roundel with flowers carved above inscription.

IN MEMORY OF
WILLIAM GREEN.
WHO DIED NOV 29 1878
AGED 63 YEARS.
ALSO OF MARTHA HIS WIFE
WHO DIED MARCH 9 1888
AGED 73 YEARS
THEY ARE NOT LOST BUT GONE BEFORE US
LET THEM NEVER BE FORGOT.
SWEET IS THEIR MEMORY TO THE LONELY
IN OUR HEARTS THEY'LL PERISH NOT
THEY REST FROM THEIR LABOURS
ALSO OF MARY THEIR DAUGHTER
WIFE OF ISAAC BROGDEN
WHO PASSED AWAY
APRIL 14TH 1902 AGED 60 YEARS

Burial Register: A416, A484, A551.
