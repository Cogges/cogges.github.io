Double plot with 4 stone kerbs and 4 posts. Mound as a headstone with metal lettering set into the stone. A cross carved in the centre of the inscription.

IN LOVING MEMORY OF
WILLIAM GREEN,
DIED MARCH 4. 1939.
IN HIS 80TH YEAR.
ALSO OF ANN, HIS WIFE,
DIED NOV. 23. 1944,
AGED 82 YEARS.

Burial Register: A684, A707.
