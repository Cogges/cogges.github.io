Pointed top headstone on a single plinth. A cross carved above inscription.

MARY ELIZABETH SODEN
(LATE OF KIDLINGTON)
14TH SEPTEMBER 1937
IN HER 74TH YEAR.
A NOBLE DAUGHTER,
A WONDERFUL SISTER.
ALSO OF
SARAH ANN SODEN
DIED 26TH JULY 1953
AGED 86 YEARS.

Burial Register: A679, A730. Register gives year of burial as 1936 for M E Soden.
